MISSION WITH A CAUSE : MISSION GANIT 2020

Mission Ganit 2020 is first ever event which is implemented with the primary objective to incorporate Technology Blended School Curriculum(TBSC), which is an innovative approach to renovate the future of the education system in India.

The complete K-12 school STEM curriculum (NCERT/STATE) will be smartly redesigned by blending frontier technologies into the learning outcomes for each chapter and each concepts. As STEM education forms the core for Artificial Intelligence era.

We are addressing the cause and effect of the “national employment crisis” due to the fourth industrial revolution in India and “technological singularity”, the biggest threat to humanity.( 69% of Indian Jobs will be automated by 2030 ).
